Privacy Policy

LumenFocus for macOS · Effective date: 19 June 2026

LumenFocus (“the App”, “we”, “us”) is a menu-bar utility for macOS that reminds you to rest your eyes. We believe privacy is a feature. This policy describes how the App handles information. In short: the App does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data, and there are no analytics, tracking, accounts, or advertising.

1. Data Controller

The App is provided by the LumenFocus developer (“the Developer”). Because the App processes all information locally on your device and the Developer never receives it, the Developer does not act as a data controller of any personal data you generate while using the App. For privacy enquiries, contact yveluo@outlook.com.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store on our servers, or transmit any personal information. Specifically, the App does not:

  • require an account, name, email address, or sign-in;
  • contain analytics, crash-reporting, advertising, or tracking SDKs;
  • access your contacts, photos, location, camera, or microphone;
  • send your usage data, preferences, or statistics off your device.

3. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

To function, the App stores the following on your Mac only (in macOS UserDefaults and the App’s sandbox container). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the Developer:

DataPurposeRetention
Preferences (work/rest durations, smart-pause toggles, ambient sound, launch-at-login, menu-bar options)Operate the App as you configured itUntil changed or the App is removed
Eye-care statistics (daily rest counts and screen-time totals)Show your local trends and heatmapRolling ~90 days, then automatically deleted

If you use the optional CSV export, a file is written only to the location you choose; you control that file thereafter.

4. Device Permissions

  • Notifications (optional): used only to deliver a local weekly eye-care summary generated on your device. No content is sent over a network. You can disable this in macOS System Settings at any time.
  • Activity awareness: to avoid interrupting you, the App checks on-device signals such as whether a full-screen or video-call app is active, or whether the Mac is idle or locked. These checks are performed in memory, in real time, and are neither recorded nor transmitted.

5. Third Parties

The App contains no third-party code that collects data. We do not sell, rent, or share information with anyone, because we do not have it.

6. Your Rights

Regulations such as the EU/UK GDPR and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) grant rights over personal data. Because the App holds your data solely on your own device and the Developer has no access to it, you remain in full control: you may view, export (CSV), or permanently erase all App data at any time by adjusting settings or by uninstalling the App. We do not sell personal information and never have.

7. Data Security

Your data is protected by the standard security model of macOS, including app sandboxing and your device’s built-in protections. Keeping your Mac and its operating system up to date is the best way to safeguard locally stored data.

8. International Users

Because no data is transmitted off your device, there is no international transfer of personal data associated with the App.

9. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction).

10. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Material changes will be reflected here before they take effect.

11. Contact

Questions or requests regarding this policy can be sent to yveluo@outlook.com.
